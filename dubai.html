Dubai







Dubai is a city and emirate in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping. Dubai has multiple famous foods like: Uqaimat, Knafe, Camel, Turkish cocktails, Samboosa, Arabic coffee and dates, Margoogat, Chebab. Dubai has tons of places to visit like: Wild Wadi Waterpark Jumeirah, Madame Tussauds Dubai, Dubai Museum, Dubai Mall, Dubai Miracle Garden, Philippine Used Car Showroom, Jumeirah Beach, La mer beach. There are also some historic places too: Al Fahidi Historical Neighbourhood, Jumeirah Mosque, Dubai Frame and other places.
